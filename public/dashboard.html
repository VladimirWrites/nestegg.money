Edit salaries

Type each person's monthly net pay side by side; the event field notes a raise or job change. Add a range of months, or import from a spreadsheet below.

Import from a spreadsheet

In Google Sheets, select the Month column and the net amount column (⌘/Ctrl-click to grab just those two), copy, choose who it's for, and paste below. An optional 3rd column becomes the event note. Re-importing a month overwrites it.

Asset

A car, a house, anything that depreciates or carries a loan. Toggle Depreciates to have its value fall a little every day (current year live, past years locked to year-end); toggle Has a loan to auto-build an amortization schedule and subtract the outstanding balance. The net value flows into your net worth for every year you own it. Assign a Category to group it on the home screen.
